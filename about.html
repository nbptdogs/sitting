About Us

Hi, I'm Elsa, the founder of Newburyport Pet Sitting. My journey with animals began early in life, growing up with adorable bunnies and later sharing my life with four beautiful English Cream Golden Retrievers. Each of these precious companions has shaped my deep understanding and love for animals.

This business was inspired by my beloved dog, Vivi, whose picture you see here. Though she has crossed the rainbow bridge, her spirit continues to guide me in every pet I care for. Vivi taught me the profound bond between humans and their pets, and it's this understanding that I bring to every pet sitting experience.

I treat each pet as if they were my own, providing the same love, attention, and care. Your pets aren't just animals to me - they're family members who deserve the very best care when their humans are away.

Meet Our Team

Meet Hannah, our dedicated overnight pet care specialist. Like myself, Hannah has a lifelong connection with animals, having grown up surrounded by dogs. As a proud pet parent to two dogs of her own, she brings both personal experience and genuine passion to her role. Hannah's natural ability to connect with animals and her attentive, caring nature make her the perfect person to keep your pets comfortable and happy during overnight stays.
